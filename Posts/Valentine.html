Nice there aren't too many ports. We have http, https, and ssh. Lets look at the webapp and start gobuster.

After a reverse search, i t turns out the heart in the background of the photo is the logo for the vulnerability heartbleed. According to heartbleed.com, "This weakness allows stealing the information protected, under normal conditions, by the SSL/TLS encryption used to secure the Internet." Let's see if there are any vulnerabilities for this.
